Idanre Hill, an awesome and beautiful natural landscape in Nigeria located at Ondo state situated approximately 3,000 feet above sea level. It's known for its diverse and variegated ecosystems, including various flora and fauna, and is home to numerous cultural sites like the Owa's Palace, shrines, and burial grounds.Idanre Hill is considered a significant cultural landscape and has been nominated for the UNESCO World Heritage Tentative List. Its unique combination of natural beauty and cultural heritage makes it a popular tourist destination. The hill is accessible by foot, with 682 steps to climb to reach the top.
